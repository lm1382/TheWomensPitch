Our Mission at The Women's Pitch

The Women's Pitch is dedicated to promoting women's football, raising awareness of key issues affecting the sport, and encouraging greater engagement at all levels.

We aim to provide a platform that informs, inspires, and empowers fans, players, and advocates for women's football.

Please explore our website and learn more about the game, its history and how you can support.
